What’s a MOO business card?

MOO is a digital company that loves to print and their passion for using digital tools to make real things happen appeals greatly to our own philosophies.

With an ever expanding product range, from business cards and MiniCards to postcards, stickers and more, MOO are constantly developing their site and services with tools that plug into other site like Bebo, Etsy, Facebook Flickr.

What’s a Flickr stream?

Flickr positions itself as “almost certainly the best online photo management and sharing application in the world”. While we’re not qualified to totally confirm such a claim, we can say it’s our favourite and preferred method of quickly and easily sharing images online.

This card project for example was based on the fact that we each have our own Flickr accounts, not to mention another for With Associates as a whole.
